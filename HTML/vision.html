Deep-Dive: The Economic Framework

To ensure sustainable growth, our economic policy centers on transparency and market diversification. We recognize that the global landscape is shifting toward digital-first economies, and we are prepared to lead this transition.

Key Objectives:

  • Establishment of the National Innovation Fund to support tech startups.
  • Reduction of bureaucratic hurdles for international trade partners.
  • Investment in green energy to power future industrial zones.
  • Comprehensive reform of the national taxation system.
  • Job training programs for the transition to automation.
  • Strategic infrastructure dev in rural growth corridors.

Modernizing the Minds of Tomorrow

Our educational reform is not merely about funding; it is about rewriting the curriculum to reflect the demands of the 21st century. Every student deserves access to high-quality technical and critical-thinking resources.

Phase I

Digital Literacy integration in all public schools by Q4 2024.

Phase II

Full modernization of vocational training centers for industrial readiness.
